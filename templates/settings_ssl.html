Certificate Requirements

For production use, you should use a certificate signed by a trusted Certificate Authority (CA). Self-signed certificates will show browser warnings.

Supported Formats

  • Certificate: PEM format (.crt or .pem)
  • Private Key: PEM format, unencrypted (.key or .pem)
